Curriculum Vitae

The structure of real materials changes over time, via complex rearrangements of their constantly jiggling atoms. We develop theoretical and computational approaches to discover these mechanisms and make connections to mechanical and thermodynamic properties.

A particular focus is designing autonomous methods that can harness the power of massively parallel computers, using statistical measures of sampling uncertainty and careful use of data-efficient machine learning approaches.
